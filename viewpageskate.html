Skateboarders Of Nepal :

Covered by Ronish Dahal, Feb 3, 2024

Art & Culture


Skate Jatra, an annual skateboard festival, recently took place in Butwal, Nepal. This exciting event showcased the growing skateboarding culture in the region, bringing together enthusiasts from various parts of South Asia. Organized by local initiatives and in collaboration with skateboarding communities, the festival included the 5th South Asian Skateboard Competition. The competition, held in Kathmandu, witnessed participants displaying their skills and competing for top honors. Nepal dominated the competition, securing an impressive eight out of the nine medals offered. This triumph not only highlighted the prowess of Nepali skateboarders but also emphasized the growing talent pool in the country. Skate Jatra has become a significant platform for skateboarders to not only compete but also to celebrate the spirit of skateboarding. Beyond the competition, the festival fosters a sense of community, encouraging collaboration and the sharing of experiences among skateboarders. The event isn't just about sports; it's a cultural celebration that appreciates the artistry and dedication of skateboarders, promoting a healthy and vibrant community of skateboard enthusiasts in Nepal.
